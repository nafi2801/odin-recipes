Tiramisu

Indulge in this classic Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cream, and a dusting of cocoa powder. A perfect balance of rich flavors and smooth textures in every bite!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 cup Espresso
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla Extract
  • 5 Egg Yolks
  • 1/2 cup White Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 16 ounces Mascarpone Cheese
  • 1.75 cups Heavy Cream
  • 28 Ladyfingers

Steps

  1. Whisk together the cocoa powder, espresso and vanilla in a bowl and set aside to really infuse the flavors together.
  2. In a mixer or using a hand mixer, beat the egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add in the salt, mascarpone cheese and continue to whip until smooth.
  4. Add the chilled cream and continue to whip, until light, creamy and smooth
  5. Make sure it can hold a soft peak.
  6. Dunk each ladyfinger in the espresso mixture to absorb the liquid and start to line a 7x11 oval baking dish with the cookies.
  7. Top the first layer of cookies with Â½ of the whipped mascarpone mixture and use the back of a metal spoon to spread it into an even layer. Dust with cocoa powder.
  8. Repeat for another layer and then finish by dusting with cocoa powder on top.
  9. Cover with plastic wrap and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.

Extra Tips

  • You can store tiramisu covered with plastic wrap for 3-4 days.
  • ALL the ingredients for the filling must be cold.
  • Mascarpone is essential. There is no substitution for it.
  • No espresso on hand - no problem! Use coffee!

Yogurt Berry Parfait

A creamy yogurt parfait layered with juicy berries and a crunchy topping â€” simple, fresh, and sweet in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Plain or Vanilla Yogurt
  • Mixed Berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/3 Avocado
  • Granola or crushed biscuits (optional)

Steps

  1. Spoon yogurt into a cup or bowl.
  2. Add a layer of berries.
  3. Sprinkle granola or crushed biscuits on top.
  4. Repeat layers if you're feelin' fancy.

Extra Tips

  • Adding a teaspoon of vinegar to the water before boiling helps the eggs stay together in the water.
  • Most of the fats in this recipe (from the avocado and the eggs) are healthy in moderation.
  • If you want to reduce the fats, just scale back on the avo or use egg whites in place of eggs.
